About me

Hello, my name is Nat Aguilera and I’m an Audiovisual Communication graduate.

I knew that I wanted to devote my life to UX/UI design while working as a content manager on a tourist apartment enterprise. I used to go crazy working with the back offices of Aribnb and HomeAway…

As I didn’t have enough money to pay for a decent UX/UI course, I started to study a free code course (500h of html and css) to prepare myself to talk to the developers while working as a UX/UI designer.

While doing this, I heard about Wallapop scholarships to study UX/UI design at Ironhack. I applied and I won a half scholarship, so when I finished my code course I started studying UX/UI design at Ironhack Barcelona on November 2018.

On my free time I do music videos and watch lots of films. You can also see me enjoying a cold beer on a music show.
